About Tacadang

Tacadang is a remote, mountainous barangay in Kibungan, Benguet, known for its breathtaking, rugged scenery, including the "Crying Mountain" and rice terraces. As a, often called a hidden gem, it features steep off-road journeys, making it a popular destination for adventure trekking, hiking, and 4x4, off-road riding.

🏕️ Tips for the Itinerary

1: Best Season: Dry season (November–April) for clear skies and optimal sea of clouds.

2: Transportation: 4x4 vehicles recommended due to rugged mountain roads.

3: Gear: Hiking shoes, flashlight, water, snacks, rain gear, camera.

4: Camping: Ask locals for safe spots; bring sleeping bags and warm layers.

5: Guides: Hiring a local guide is recommended for navigation and cultural insights.

6: Respect: Ask before photographing locals or farms and leave no litter.
